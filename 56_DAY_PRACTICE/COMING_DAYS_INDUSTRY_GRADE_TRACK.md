## Interview leverage format (use daily)

- Situation: What you were building
- Failure: What broke and why it mattered
- Debug: How you diagnosed root cause
- Fix: What you changed
- Prevention: What you added to stop recurrence
- Impact: What improved (latency/error/correctness)

## Rule

Do not skip debugging notes. They are your experience multiplier.
