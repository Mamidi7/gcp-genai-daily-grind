RBI Policy Rates (May 2026)

These appear in almost every GA paper. Memorize the numbers.
Rate/ToolCurrent (May 2026)What It Does
Repo Rate6.00%RBI lends to banks (short term)
Reverse Repo3.35%Banks park excess funds with RBI
Bank Rate6.25%Long-term lending rate to banks
MSF (Marginal Standing Facility)6.25%Emergency overnight borrowing
SDER (Standing Deposit Facility)5.00%Replaced reverse repo as floor rate
CRR4.00%Cash banks must hold with RBI
SLR18.00%Statutory liquid assets (bonds, gold, cash)
Trap: Bank Rate and MSF are the SAME (6.25%). Many students confuse them. Bank Rate = long term. MSF = overnight emergency. Different purpose, same number.
Trap: SDER (5.00%) is the NEW floor rate since April 2022. Reverse repo (3.35%) still exists but SDER is the active floor in monetary policy framework. If asked "floor rate of LAF corridor" — answer is SDER, not reverse repo.

03

Banking Terms — Exam Favorites

One-line definitions. No depth needed for Phase 1.
NPA
Non-Performing Asset. Loan unpaid for 90 days. Substandard (90-365d), Doubtful (1-3y), Loss (>3y).
PCR
Provision Coverage Ratio. Minimum 70% for NPA. Higher = better.
SARFAESI Act
Securitization and Reconstruction. Banks can attach property without court for NPAs > 20 lakh. Year: 2002.
IBC
Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code. 2016. Resolution in 330 days. NCLT handles.
ARC
Asset Reconstruction Company. Buys bad loans from banks. Regulated by RBI.
PCA
Prompt Corrective Action. RBI imposes on weak banks (3 triggers: capital, asset quality, profitability). Restrictions on lending, branch expansion, dividends.
CAR / CRAR
Capital to Risk-weighted Assets Ratio. RBI minimum: 9%. Basel: 8%.
CDR
Corporate Debt Restructuring. For viable units facing temporary problems.
5/25 Scheme
Long-term infra loans can be restructured every 5 years, max 25 years. For viable projects.
S4A
Scheme for Sustainable Structuring of Stressed Assets. For large projects (>500cr). Viable portion = standard, unviable = NPA.
Q. Under IBC 2016, what is the maximum time allowed for resolution of a corporate insolvency case?
A) 180 daysB) 270 days C) 330 days D) 365 days
Initial 180 days + 90 days extension = 330 days total. Common trap: 180 days is initial period only.

Q. What is the maximum transaction limit for UPI per transaction?
A) 50,000B) 1 lakh C) 2 lakh D) 5 lakh
UPI limit is Rs 2 lakh per transaction. For new users, it's Rs 1 lakh for first 24 hours. IMPS limit is Rs 5 lakh. Don't confuse the two.

Recent RBI Changes (2024-26)

Current affairs + banking overlap. Most important for Phase 1.
SDER introduced
April 2022. Standing Deposit Facility Rate = 5.00%. New floor for LAF corridor. Reverse repo still exists but SDER is the active policy floor.
UPI Lite
Offline UPI payments. No internet needed. Wallet-based. Max balance 2,000, per transaction 500.
UPI 123Pay
UPI for feature phones. Dial *99# or use voice. No smartphone needed.
Credit Card linking to UPI
RBI allowed RuPay credit cards on UPI since June 2022. Visa/Mastercard not yet.
e-RUPI
Aug 2021. Prepaid voucher for specific purpose. Person + purpose specific. No bank account needed for beneficiary.
Tokenization
From Oct 2022, merchants cannot store actual card numbers. Must use tokens. Enhances security.
CBDC Retail Pilot
Launched Dec 2022. 15 banks participating. Digital wallet for e-Rupee. Offline capability tested.
Repo rate cuts
Feb 2025: 25 bps cut to 6.25%. April 2025: 25 bps cut to 6.00%. Current rate = 6.00% as of May 2026.

Insurance & Pension

1-2 questions. Quick recall only.
Bima Sugam
Proposed unified insurance platform. IRDAI initiative. One-stop for buying, claims, grievances. Launch expected soon.
Insurance Penetration (India)
~4.2% (very low). Global avg ~7%. Life insurance dominates. Health growing fast.
NPS
National Pension System. PFRDA regulates. Mandatory for central govt employees (from 2004). Voluntary for others. Partial withdrawal after 3 years.
APY
Atal Pension Yojana. 2015. Guaranteed pension 1,000-5,000/month from age 60. Target: unorganized sector. Govt co-contributes 50% or 1,000/year (whichever is lower) for 5 years.
OPS vs NPS
OPS = Old Pension Scheme (guaranteed 50% of last drawn salary). NPS = market-linked. Some states reverting to OPS. Central govt still on NPS.

Stock Market Basics

1-2 questions. Definitions only.
Sensex
BSE index. 30 companies. Free-float market cap weighted. Base year 1978-79 = 100.
Nifty 50
NSE index. 50 companies. Free-float market cap weighted. Base year 1995 = 1000.
FII vs FPI
FII = Foreign Institutional Investor (old term). FPI = Foreign Portfolio Investor (new term, 2014 SEBI norms). Same thing, different name.
IPO vs FPO
IPO = First sale by private company going public. FPO = Follow-on Public Offer. Additional shares by already listed company.
Demat Account
Holds shares electronically. Depositories: CDSL and NSDL. SEBI regulates.
Stock Exchange
BSE (1875, oldest Asia). NSE (1992, first demutualized). SEBI regulates both.
