## Errors Debugged

- **Pydantic 422 vs handler 500 separation:** Tested empty body → 422 (Pydantic rejects before handler), intentionally broken handler → 500. Confirmed validation and business logic are separate layers in FastAPI. Flask equivalent would require manual `request.get_json()` + `if/else` for both cases.
