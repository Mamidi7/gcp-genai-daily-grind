05

Section strategy

How to approach each section on exam day.

General Awareness — 80 marks (make or break)

This decides your result. Daily 45 min non-negotiable. Current Affairs (50% of GA), Banking Awareness (25%), Static GK (15%), Schemes (10%). Bot delivers daily curated news at 7 AM.

Reasoning — 60 marks (your growth area)

CA students find reasoning new, but it's highly learnable. Puzzles + Seating Arrangement = 30+ marks alone. Syllogism + Inequality are free marks — target 100% accuracy. 20 Qs daily.

Quantitative Aptitude — 30 marks (your strength)

Your CA math is stronger. Problem is speed, not concepts. DI is king — 2-3 sets daily. Arithmetic formula revision sheet. Accuracy over attempts.

English — 30 marks (don't neglect)

RC = 10 marks — practice speed reading. Error Spotting + Cloze are pattern-based. Para Jumbles needs practice. Don't take it for granted.
